Hilads / Brand Identity

Logo concepts — "H¡" direction

Concept 01
H¡ — The Bold Greeting
Filled icon. The "¡" is treated as a greeting mark — not punctuation. Gradient background, white letterforms. The dot is oversized and warm, reinforcing "live presence." Works best as app icon and favicon.
Icon — dark
Icon — light
Wordmark — dark
Animation idea: The dot slowly pulses between full opacity and ~60%, like a heartbeat. Subtle breathing rhythm (1.8s loop). Reinforces "live city" — someone is always there.
Concept 02
Constructed — The Geometric Mark
No filled background. The letterforms are built from rectangles and a circle — purely geometric, like a system icon. Gradient applied directly to the shapes. Very scalable. Works on any background.
Icon — dark
Icon — light
Wordmark — dark
Animation idea: The dot floats up 3px and back, very gently (2.5s loop). Like a bubble rising. Feels light and social — not an alarm, just presence.
Concept 03
The Dot Mark — Minimal Signal
The most stripped-down. The icon is just "¡" as a pure geometric shape — a large dot with a short stroke below it. Reads as a signal, a ping, a live indicator. Doubles as a notification badge. The wordmark uses the mark inline with "hilads."
Icon — dark
Icon — light
Wordmark — dark
Animation idea: Ripple rings expand outward from the dot (like a WiFi / sonar ping). 2 rings, 3s loop, fade out as they grow. Pure "live signal" metaphor — city is broadcasting, you just tuned in.
