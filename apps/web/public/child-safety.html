Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026

Hilads is committed to ensuring user safety and preventing any form of child exploitation or abuse on our platform. The protection of minors is a non-negotiable priority.

Our app is designed for adults (18 and older) and does not target children in its features, content, or marketing.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Hilads and will be removed immediately upon detection or report:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors
  • Images, videos, or text that depict or solicit minors in a harmful context
  • Any attempt to contact, groom, or exploit users who are or may be minors

Violations of this policy will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

Safety Measures

We actively enforce the following protections:

  • Content reporting - any user can report inappropriate content directly from the app
  • User blocking - any user can block another user at any time
  • Active moderation - reported content is reviewed and acted upon promptly
  • Account removal - accounts found in violation of these standards are permanently removed
